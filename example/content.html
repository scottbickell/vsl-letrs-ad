Elementary Grades

Proven professional development for
teachers of language and literacy

The harsh reality is that approximately 31% of fourth graders cannot read at even “basic” levels.

Teachers matter more to these students’ futures than any other aspect of schooling. However, many teachers are not well prepared to teach all students to read, including those who struggle.

Teachers need professional development that provides deeper knowledge, skills and practice in delivering better literacy instruction.

LETRS fills in the gaps in teacher preparation. It’s a course of study that addresses the structures of English language, the cognitive processes of learning to read, and the teaching practices proven to be effective in preventing and remediating reading difficulties.

LETRS:

  • Integrates research-supported practices to deepen educator knowledge
  • Addresses the five essential components of reading
  • Empowers educators to successfully meet standards-based outcomes
  • Improves literacy instruction—regardless of chosen curriculum
  • Has a proven track record of success in districts across the country

LETRS empowers teachers to understand the what, why, and how of scientifically based reading instruction.
